    Custom Professions

    A custom profession is a snapshot of one dwarf's currently-enabled labors. Select a dwarf (click a row in the grid), give the snapshot a name, and click Save from selected. To use it, select a different dwarf and press apply next to the saved entry — it queues labor toggles to match the snapshot plus a profession-name change. Nothing is sent to DF until you press Commit in the toolbar. Stored only in this browser.
